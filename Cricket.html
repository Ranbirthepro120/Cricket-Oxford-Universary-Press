About

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the centre of which is a 22-yard pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps!




ICC World Cup

The International Cricket Council is the world governing body of cricket. It was founded as the Imperial Cricket Conference in 1909 by representatives from Australia, England and South Africa. It was renamed as the International Cricket Conference in 1965, and took up its current name in 1989!




Indian Premier League (IPL)

The 2020 Indian Premier League, also known as IPL 13 and branded as Dream11 Indian Premier League 2020, was the thirteenth season of the IPL, a professional Twenty20 cricket league established by the Board of Control for Cricket in India in 2007. Mumbai Indians (Founded in 2008) is by far the Best Team in the IPL winning 5 Titles throughout their Franchise!
